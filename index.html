About Our Hotel

Founded in 2010 by Kudakwashe Chiudzu, Kudah Hotel has grown from a small boutique establishment to one of the most prestigious hotels in the region. Our commitment to excellence has earned us numerous awards and the loyalty of guests from around the world.

With 120 luxurious rooms and suites, two gourmet restaurants, a full-service spa, and state-of-the-art conference facilities, we cater to both leisure and business travelers.

Our dedicated staff is committed to ensuring your stay is nothing short of perfect, with personalized service and attention to detail that sets us apart.
